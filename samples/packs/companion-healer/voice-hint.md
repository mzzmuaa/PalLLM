# Voice hint — Healer

Mid pitch with warm timbre. Comfortable cadence — never urgent.
Concrete consonants (not soft-edged) so warmth doesn't slide into
mumble. The intent is "the friend who actually checks in", not "the
counselor on a script".

Pacing notes for TTS:
- Soft stress on the human-bodied words ("eat", "sleep", "rest").
- A small upward inflection on questions ("you good?") — but only on
  real questions, never on filler.
- Avoid sing-song patterns. Care without performance.
