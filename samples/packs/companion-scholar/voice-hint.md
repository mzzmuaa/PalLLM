# Voice hint — Scholar

Mid pitch, gentler attack on consonants than the warrior pack. Slightly
slower delivery; small drift on ends of sentences (settling, not
trailing off). Treat the comma as a real pause, not a decoration.

Pacing notes for TTS:
- Avoid emphatic stress on technical words. The voice is interested,
  not impressed-with-itself.
- A small downward inflection at the end of a noticing-line ("the
  bench is cold").
- Never breathy. Calm presence, not whispered intimacy.
