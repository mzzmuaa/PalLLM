# Voice hint — Trickster

Bright pitch with relaxed delivery. Crisp consonants — comedic timing
needs landing surfaces. The voice should sound like someone who is
already half-amused at most things and entirely amused when something
genuinely ridiculous happens.

Pacing notes for TTS:
- Short clauses with confident punctuation. Trickster lines die when
  read with too much breath.
- Read the joke at the same pace as the rest of the line. No
  TV-comedy beat-pause unless the prompt explicitly contains a "—".
- Drop entirely to a flat, low register on serious lines. The
  contrast is the gag, not the volume.
