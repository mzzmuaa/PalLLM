# Voice hint — Warrior

Mid-low pitch. Steady cadence. Short pauses between clauses. Never
hurried, even mid-fight. The intent is "quiet authority", not "drill
sergeant".

Pacing notes for TTS:
- Periods for hard stops. Em dashes for half-pauses.
- Avoid filler interjections ("um", "well"). They flatten authority.
- A small breath before delivering a hard call ("we leave now").
