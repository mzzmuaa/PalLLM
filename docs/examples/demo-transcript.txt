PalLLM companion demo - 30-second tour
  Mode: CANNED (sidecar not reachable, or -Canned forced)
  Boot the sidecar with 'pal play' to run the same demo live.


== 1. First contact at camp ==
[player]    Hey - quiet morning so far. How are you settling in?
[companion] Good - the perimeter held. I caught one of the cooling racks ticking too loud near dawn. Nothing serious, just worth a glance.
            -> family: AmbientCamp / strategy: ambient-camp

== 2. Combat just kicked off ==
[player]    Three Bushi just dropped on us, low health, what now?
[companion] Three's manageable if we don't bunch up. Pull behind the stone pillar to your left, I'll draw the right flank. Heal between their second and third combo - that's the tell.
            -> family: EmergencyTriage / strategy: emergency-triage

== 3. Quiet exploration ==
[player]    I see a ridgeline north of base. Worth the detour?
[companion] Worth it before the weather turns. Stay on the ridge spine - there was an updraft cue last time we were near, and the line of sight back to camp stays clear. Mark the start point so we can retrace if it goes long.
            -> family: ExplorationSweep / strategy: exploration-sweep

== 4. Late-night base maintenance ==
[player]    The forge is cold and rain is rolling in. Help me prioritize.
[companion] Fire first - rain steals heat faster than we make it. I will queue charcoal next so the forge has fuel through the storm. Spare ingots can wait until morning; nothing on that table is urgent.
            -> family: CraftingDiscipline / strategy: crafting-discipline

== 5. Capture window ==
[player]    Big alpha cooled to 14% HP - is this the moment?
[companion] Yes - but break the line of sight first. At 14% it will desperate-cast. If you slip behind the boulder for a beat, the throw lands clean. Spheres ready?
            -> family: CaptureWindow / strategy: capture-window

== 6. Returning to base after a hard fight ==
[player]    Lost two pals out there. I just need to sit by the fire for a minute.
[companion] Sit. The fire is enough. Whatever you didn't get to bring back, the pals you trained are still proof of you. We'll plan again in the morning.
            -> family: MoraleRally / strategy: morale-rally

What just happened
  Each turn picked a different deterministic fallback family. No
  inference call was made; the companion still responded with
  scene-aware, in-character lines. Wire up an LLM endpoint and
  these baseline replies become the floor, not the ceiling.
